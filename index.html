The Physics & Astronomy Club, IIT Delhi

from quarks to quasars !

Through this club, we aim to create an engaging environment where scientific thought and logical reasoning is encouraged to tackle both previously solved and unsolved questions regarding the universe and the laws of physics.

We focusses to gather enthusiasts and facilitate knowledge sharing across all groups of students and to capture the interest of all the students in the mysteries of the universe. We also promotes learning from the experts of the Research field and the people in the industry

Our History

The Physics and Astronomy Club IIT Delhi, is the successor to the Astronomy Club of Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi, which had been established in 2011, and had been operational under Technocracy, CAIC, IIT Delhi

What do we do?

Sky observations:
Both in-campus and short excursions for observation of the night sky, guided by either members from the club or professional guides to learn to observe, identify and deduce the properties of objects visible in the night sky through a telescope.

Guest Lectures and Workshops:
Lectures and workshops on several topics in Physics, Astronomy and Mathematics by faculty from colleges and research institutes from across India and abroad.

Discussions:
Open discussions on recent and/or important topics in Physics, Mathematics and Astronomy between members of the club and guests, may or may not be aided by a professor

Competitive Events:
Prepare for and participate in inter-college and interinstitution events in the fields of Physics, Astronomy, Mathematics, Design and Robotics.

Miscellaneous:
Includes competitive quizzes, treasure hunts, research projects, comic design and more.
